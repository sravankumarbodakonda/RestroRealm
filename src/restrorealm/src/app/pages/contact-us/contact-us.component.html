Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we strongly recommend making reservations, especially for weekend dining. You can make a reservation online or by calling (555) 123-4568.

Is there parking available?

We offer complimentary valet parking for our guests. There is also a public parking garage located one block away.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when making your reservation, and our chefs will be happy to accommodate your needs.

Can you host private events?

Yes, we have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 30 guests. For larger events, the entire restaurant can be reserved. Please contact our events team for more information.
